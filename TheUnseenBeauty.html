THE UNSEEN BEAUTY 2021

Gorche paint and watercolor on paper. 37cm x 41.5cm.

Got inspired from widely colourful coral reefs hidden underneath the ocean water and swathes of wildflower meadows quietly bloom beneath mountain peaks, I used gouache paint and watercolor to depict their natural beauty which remain out of human sight most of the time.
